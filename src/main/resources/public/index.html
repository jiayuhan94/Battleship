Instructions

  1. Choose a ship, then select it's starting coordinates and orientation.
  2. Click "Submit" once you placed all 5 of your ships.
  3. Type in coordinates that you want to scan or fire on(1-10).
  4. Scan can only be used 3 times per game. It will turn black after all scans have been used.
  5. Using scan will take up a turn.
  6. Yellow = your ship, Red = hit, Green = miss.
  7. Win = sunk all enemy`s ships, Lost = all of your ships got hit.
  8. Press "New game" to reset the board and start a new game.
